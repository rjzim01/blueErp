# blueERP V3 Revamp Plan
# Generated: April 2026

## WHAT IS blueERP?

blueERP is an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system for a business that includes:
- **Customers** - Customer management
- **Products** - Product catalog with colors, sizes, packaging
- **Stock** - Inventory management
- **Orders** - Order processing
- **POS** - Point of Sale system
- **Employees** - Staff management
- **Payroll** - Salary calculations
- **Balance** - Financial management
- **Reports** - Business reports

5. **DBBL Balance** - from balance table
6. **Islami Bank Balance** - from balance table

2. **Total Available Barcodes** - current_stock count (links to auditdetails)

## APIs Used by Dashboard
- `GET /api/v1/cdn/common/dashboard` - Main dashboard data
- `GET /api/v1/cdn/common/dashboard-range/{from}/{to}` - Range data
- `GET /api/v1/payroll/punch` - Punch attendance
- `GET /api/v1/payroll/lastpunch` - Last punch status
- `POST /api/v1/utility/barcode/makedamage` - Mark barcode damaged
- `POST /api/v1/utility/barcode/makesellable` - Make barcode sellable
